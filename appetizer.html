Dim Chop

Dim Chop is a popular Bangladeshi appetizer that features hard-boiled eggs coated in a spiced chickpea flour batter and deep-fried to crispy perfection. With its golden exterior and savory filling, Dim Chop offers a flavorful and satisfying introduction to the world of Bangladeshi snacking delights
Keema Porota

Keema Porota is a mouthwatering Bangladeshi dish that combines minced meat, typically beef or mutton, with a medley of aromatic spices, and is enveloped in layers of flaky, pan-fried flatbread. This flavorful and hearty combination delivers a delightful experience of tender meat and well-seasoned bread, showcasing the essence of comfort food in Bangladeshi cuisine
