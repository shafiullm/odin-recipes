Dessert Recipes

Image Name Description
Roshmalai

Roshmalai is a beloved Bangladeshi dessert featuring delicate, soft paneer (cottage cheese) balls soaked in fragrant milk infused with saffron and cardamom. This dessert is a perfect blend of textures and flavors, offering a sublime experience of creamy sweetness with a touch of aromatic richness
Shemai

Shemai is a traditional Bangladeshi dessert made from thin vermicelli noodles, gently simmered in sweetened milk and adorned with nuts and dried fruits. This delectable treat offers a harmonious balance of tender noodles and rich, creamy flavors, creating a comforting dessert that's both soothing and indulgent.
