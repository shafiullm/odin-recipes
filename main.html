Chicken Kacchi Biryani

Chicken Kacchi Biryani is a sumptuous Bangladeshi delicacy that marries fragrant basmati rice and succulent marinated chicken, cooked together in a symphony of spices, saffron, and caramelized onions. This classic dish embodies the essence of celebration and tradition, offering a delightful fusion of flavors and tender textures that define the art of Biryani.
